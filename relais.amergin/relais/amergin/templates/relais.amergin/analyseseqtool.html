Upload a sequence for analysis. Data will be cleaned up and reformated into a canonical form before use. The allowed symbols are the IUPAC codes. Question marks are translated to ambiguous characters ('N'). Gaps are represented by '-'. Any whitespace, blank spaces etc. are stripped.

A sequence file can be uploaded instead. Formats understood (and the corresponding file extension) are genbank (.genbank, .gb), fasta (.fasta, .fas), phylip (.phy, .phylip) and stockholm (.sth).
